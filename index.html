Pharmaceutical quality & regulatory

Two tools for audit planning and regulatory documentation

AuditPilot and the CTD Stability Document Generator help quality and regulatory affairs teams in pharma and clinical settings cut down the time spent on audit planning and report writing. Both are designed to work within GxP requirements.

The tools

Each one takes on a part of the quality and regulatory workload that normally takes a lot of manual effort.

Audit planning

AuditPilot

AuditPilot helps quality teams decide what to audit, when, and by whom. It ranks auditable units by risk, drafts the annual plan, and keeps the records needed for inspections.

  • Risk score and current control status for each auditable unit
  • Alerts when regulations change or an audit runs over schedule
  • Annual audit plans across sites and facilities
  • Auditor assignments balanced across the team
  • Site overview with audit trails for 21 CFR Part 11

Regulatory submissions

CTD Stability Document Generator

This tool builds the stability section of a Common Technical Document from your study data, so regulatory affairs teams no longer have to assemble it by hand.

  • Builds the stability section for CTD Module 3.2.P.8
  • Reads stability study data and formats it into the report
  • Less time spent drafting and reformatting
  • Follows the structure expected for ICH and CTD filings
  • Consistent output, with fewer transcription errors
